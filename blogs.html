Al-Kabsa - A Main Dish In Saudia Arabia

"Al-Kabsa" is a popular Saudi Arabian dish that is mainly composed of rice, minced meat, chicken or small pieces of meat, along with onions, garlic, and various spices such as cardamom, saffron, cumin, black pepper, and coriander. Typically, Al-Kabsa is served in a large pot on the table and decorated with nuts, raisins, and fried onions.

Ma'soub with Honey

"Ma'soub with Honey" is a famous traditional Saudi dessert, consisting of dough filled with dates, nuts, and spices, and covered with delicious honey. Ma'soub with Honey is a popular dessert that is prepared during the holy month of Ramadan and special occasions.

How to make Al-Marqooq

"Al-Marqooq" is a popular dish in Saudi and Gulf cuisine that consists of thin layers of dough saturated with oil, which are then browned to a golden and crispy texture. Al-Marqooq is usually served as a light snack with tea or coffee, and can be eaten alone or with hummus or salty cheese dip.

Modus operandi of Saudi champagne juice

"Champagne Saudi" does not refer to a specific or recognized type of champagne or sparkling wine produced in Saudi Arabia. The production and sale of alcohol is prohibited in Saudi Arabia due to religious and cultural reasons. Therefore, there is no champagne or sparkling wine produced in the country. However, there are non-alcoholic sparkling beverages and mocktails that are popular in Saudi Arabia and can be enjoyed in many restaurants, cafes, and events.

How to make Saudi Ma'soub

Saudi Ma'soub with Honey is known for its unique and distinct flavor, where the dates and nuts are mixed with spices such as cardamom, saffron, cinnamon, and ginger to give it its distinctive taste. The dough is then wrapped around the filling and baked in the oven, and finally dipped in warm honey. Ma'soub with Honey can be bought ready-made in markets and shops, but it can also be prepared at home using different recipes that contain different ingredients. Ma'soub with Honey is a delicious and favorite dessert for many in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
