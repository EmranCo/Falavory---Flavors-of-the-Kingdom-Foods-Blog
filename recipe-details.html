Al-Kabsa - A Main Dish In Saudia Arabia

13 Steps Posted by Amal Najmi
50 mins

Ingredients

For the meat:
  • 2 lbs. of chicken or lamb meat, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cardamom
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 4 cups of water
For the rice:
  • 2 cups of basmati rice, rinsed and drained
  • 4 cups of chicken or lamb broth (from cooking the meat)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cardamom
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of raisins (optional)
  • 1/2 cup of slivered almonds (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute.
  3. Add the meat to the pot and cook until it is browned on all sides.
  4. Add the ground cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom, salt, and black pepper to the pot and stir well.
  5. Add the water to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the meat simmer for about 1 hour or until it is tender and cooked through.
  6. Once the meat is cooked, remove it from the pot and set it aside. Reserve the broth for cooking the rice.
  7. In another pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent.
  8. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute.
  9. Add the rice to the pot and stir well to coat it with the oil.
  10. Add the broth to the pot and stir well. Add the ground cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom, and salt, and stir again.
  11. Bring the rice to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until it is fully cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  12. Once the rice is cooked, add the raisins and slivered almonds (if using) and stir gently.
  13. To serve, place the rice on a large platter and arrange the meat on top. Garnish with additional raisins and almonds, if desired.
